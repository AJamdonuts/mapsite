Super-diversity is an example of a broader issue that Variable Mapper aims to address. Understanding urban socioeconomic dynamics demands tackling complex interdependencies using large multidimensional spatial datasets, which typically requires deep statistical expertise. This complexity poses significant obstacles for policymakers attempting to make informed decisions on urban policy and has a substantial influence on investments in infrastructure and impacting large populations.
