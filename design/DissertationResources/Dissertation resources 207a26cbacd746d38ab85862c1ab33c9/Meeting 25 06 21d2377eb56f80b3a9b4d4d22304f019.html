Tested rendering tree dataset in both Leaflet and MapLibre. While Leaflet was simpler, MapLibre offered smoother zoom and better performance with larger point data.

Leaflet:

  • Easy to use
  • Popular
  • More for small projects
  • Struggles with large datasets

MapLibre GL JS

  • Supports vector tiles, custom basemaps
  • fast performance
  • 3D features

Reviewed tech blogs, academic papers, and GIS community feedback. Leaflet remains the simplest and most downloaded tool, MapLibre GL is the top open-source WebGL mapping library. Deck.gl is used for dataset analytics, and Cesium is best for 3D use.
